## Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 14, 2026

### 1. Information We Collect
We may collect information you provide directly, such as your email address when subscribing to updates. We also collect standard web analytics data (page views, referral sources) through privacy-respecting analytics tools.

### 2. How We Use Your Information
We use collected information to:
- Deliver and improve our content and services
- Send updates if you have subscribed
- Authenticate with third-party platforms (TikTok, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, etc.) for content publishing

### 3. Third-Party Services
Our website and services integrate with third-party platforms for content syndication. When you interact with our content on these platforms, their respective privacy policies apply:
- TikTok: https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
- Twitter/X: https://twitter.com/en/privacy
- LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/legal/privacy-policy

### 4. TikTok Integration
We use TikTok's Content Posting API to publish video content. We access only the permissions necessary to upload and publish content. We do not collect or store TikTok user data beyond what is required for authentication.

### 5. Data Retention
We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy.

### 6. Data Security
We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

### 7. Your Rights
You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us.

### 8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

### 9. Contact
For privacy-related inquiries, contact vedang@veda.ng.
